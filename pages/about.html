Our Story, Your Seafood

Built on relationships with fishermen, farmers, and global buyers, our story is a journey of quality, sustainability, and dedication to the ocean's offerings.

Origin (To Do)

  • By initially operating as a seafood marketing and trading company, we brokered deals between processors and international buyers.
  • This gave us an understanding of market demands in various regions, from the specific species and packaging preferred by importers and wholesalers to the stringent quality standards of the European Union and USA.
  • We built a reputation for reliability and market knowledge, but we were ultimately dependent on the quality and capacity of our suppliers.

Growth Phase (InProgress)

  • To move beyond commodity trading and guarantee the consistent quality our customers expected, we made the strategic decision to transition from a marketing company to an exporter. We invested in our own pre-processing facilities, and got our products processed as a merchant exporter with direct partnerships with sustainable fishing fleets and certified aquaculture farms.
  • This allowed us to implement strict Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points (HACCP) standards and meticulously manage every step of the cold chain, from sourcing to packaging. This move transformed our business model from one of connecting buyers and sellers to one of creating branded seafood products.

Today (Done)

  • The company is a trusted seafood exporter known for its premium, sustainable products. Our marketing DNA remains at our core, but it is now powered by a fully controlled, end-to-end supply chain.
  • This unique history allows us to not only meet the demands of global markets but to anticipate them, delivering the freshness and quality that define our brand.

Company history, mission & legacy

From humble coastal beginnings to a global export force - our journey is rooted in purpose, progress, and pride.

Company History

Started in 1995, we began sourcing fresh catch from local fishermen. Over the year, we grew into a nationwide network with processing units, cold storage, and streamlined logistics — ready to serve global markets.

Mission

To supply high-quality, traceable seafood worldwide, while supporting local fishing communities and promoting sustainability in everything we do.

Legacy

With decades of experience and global partnerships, our legacy is built on trust, innovation, and commitment to responsible seafood exports.
